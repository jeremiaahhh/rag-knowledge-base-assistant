# ACME Corp — Employee Handbook (excerpt)

## Vacation Policy

Full-time employees receive 25 days of paid vacation per year, accruing
proportionally each month. Vacation days carry over into the next calendar year,
but unused balances above 10 days are paid out in March. Employees must request
vacation at least two weeks in advance for periods longer than three days, and
the People team encourages teams to coordinate so that critical projects are not
blocked during peak vacation weeks (July, August, December).

In addition to vacation, employees receive 10 paid sick days per year, which do
not carry over and are not paid out. Sick days do not require a doctor's note
unless three or more consecutive days are taken.

## Remote Work

ACME Corp operates on a remote-first model. Employees may work from any
location within their employment country, with three required in-office weeks
per year for the company offsite, the engineering summit, and the annual
all-hands. Managers may request additional in-person days for onboarding or
project kick-offs. Remote employees receive a one-time €1,500 home office
budget that can be used for ergonomic equipment.

International travel for remote work is supported up to 60 days per year
provided employees give two weeks' notice, retain a stable internet connection,
and overlap at least four hours daily with their team's working window.

## Expenses

Business expenses are reimbursed via the Pleo card or the expense portal. The
policy follows three simple rules: act in the company's best interest, spend
the company's money like it's your own, and keep a receipt. Travel bookings
should be made through Navan whenever possible. Expense reports must be
submitted within 30 days. Reimbursements are paid out twice a month, on the
1st and 15th.

## Information Security Basics

All laptops must be encrypted with FileVault or BitLocker, run an up-to-date
operating system, and have an automatic screen lock after five minutes of
inactivity. Passwords are managed through 1Password — never share a password
over Slack, email, or chat. Two-factor authentication is mandatory for all
corporate services. Suspected security incidents must be reported to
`security@acme.example` within one hour of discovery.
