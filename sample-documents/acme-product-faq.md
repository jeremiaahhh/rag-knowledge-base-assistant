# ACME — Product FAQ

## Pricing Tiers

ACME offers three pricing tiers designed for teams of every size:

- **Starter** — free for up to 3 users. Includes the core knowledge base, two
  integrations, and community support. Suited to individual developers and
  small early-stage teams evaluating the product.
- **Growth** — $29 per user per month, billed annually. Unlimited integrations,
  team workspaces, audit log retention for 90 days, and email support with a
  one-business-day response time.
- **Enterprise** — custom pricing. Adds SSO (SAML and SCIM), audit log
  retention for two years, on-prem deployment options, a dedicated customer
  success manager, and a 99.95% uptime SLA backed by service credits.

All paid tiers include a 14-day free trial with no credit card required.
Annual contracts receive a 15% discount over month-to-month billing.

## Integrations

ACME integrates with Slack, Microsoft Teams, GitHub, GitLab, Jira, Linear,
Notion, Confluence, Salesforce, HubSpot, and 30+ additional tools via our
Zapier connector. Webhooks and a public REST API are available on Growth and
Enterprise tiers. The integrations directory is updated monthly; customers
can request new integrations through the in-product feedback form.

## Support SLAs

Support tier response targets, measured during local business hours:

- Starter: best-effort community support
- Growth: 1 business day first response, 3 business days resolution target
- Enterprise: 1 hour first response for SEV-1, 4 hours for SEV-2, 24/7
  coverage, named technical account manager

Customers can open a support ticket via the in-product help menu or by
emailing `support@acme.example`. Status and incident history is published at
`status.acme.example`.

## Data & Compliance

ACME is SOC 2 Type II certified and undergoes annual penetration testing.
Customer data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 and in transit with TLS 1.2+.
Data residency in either the EU or US is configurable per workspace on the
Enterprise tier. Customers can export all their data as JSON or CSV from the
workspace settings at any time.
